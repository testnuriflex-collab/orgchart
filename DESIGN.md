# Organization Chart Studio Design System

Airbnb 계열 DESIGN.md를 HR 업무 도구에 맞게 번역한 로컬 디자인 시스템입니다.

## Tokens

- `canvas`: `#FFFFFF`
- `surface`: `#F7F7F7`
- `surfaceStrong`: `#F2F2F2`
- `ink`: `#222222`
- `body`: `#3F3F3F`
- `muted`: `#4A4A4A`
- `hairline`: `#DDDDDD`
- `accent`: `#A0002A`
- `accentActive`: `#8F0024`
- `success`: `#0F7B45`
- `warning`: `#B26A00`

## Typography

Use `Paperlogy` when bundled or installed. Fall back to `Apple SD Gothic Neo`, `Malgun Gothic`, `Inter`, and the platform sans-serif stack.

## Components

- Buttons: 8px radius, 40-48px height, accent only for primary actions.
- Inputs: white fill, 1px hairline border, 8px radius.
- Organization cards: 14px radius, white fill, hairline border, no heavy shadows.
- Layout: left command rail, central canvas, right properties panel.
